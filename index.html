This is a traditional Pacman game that consists of a pacman game board, a pacman and three ghosts. The Pacman's objective is to consume all the pellets onto the board and score maximum points possible. The Pacman player wins whenever he maximizes his score by eating all the pellets. If the Pacman collides with any of the ghosts, its a game over.

Use W A S D to move the Pacman!
